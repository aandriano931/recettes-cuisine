# Rice paper spanakopita spiral

## Ingrédients

- 3 feuilles de rice paper (standard size works best, typically 8.5-9 inches in diameter)
- 2 eggs
- 0.5 tasse de roast chicken (shredded)
- 0.5 tasse de spinach (sautéed)
- 0.5 tasse de mozzarella (shredded)
- 0.5 tasse de feta (crumbled)
- sesame seeds
- salt and pepper
- lemon (for serving)

## Préparation

1. Make the Filling: Combine chicken, spinach, mozzarella, and feta in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper and mix to combine.
2. Prep the Rice Paper: Whisk eggs on a shallow plate. Dip each rice paper sheet into the egg wash for a few seconds, then lay flat on a lightly oiled board or cutting board.
3. Roll: Spoon the filling in a line across the center of each sheet and roll tightly into a cylinder.
4. Shape: Gently curve each roll around itself to form a spiral, tucking the end underneath to hold.
5. Season and Bake: Brush the outside of each spiral generously with egg wash and sprinkle with sesame seeds. Bake at 400°F for 15–20 minutes until golden brown and crisp all over.
6. Serve: Serve warm with lemon wedges on the side.
